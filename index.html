Work

Furnitrade, Web Platform

Full-Stack Developer• Sept 2018 - Present

I collaborated in student team to design a used furniture trading platform for UC San Diego students. With our clean (students only, furniture only) and efficient (powered by built-in search enginee and price match system) web-based app, trading used furniture is no longer bothersome.
I developed using Flask framework the server-side user authentication and furniture information module, implemented APIs for client-side to interact with MongoDB, and helped implement user interface with React.js and Material UI. I also created user case, user stories and product introduction.

Recommendar System, Yelp & Amazon's Datasets

Machine Learning Engineer• Nov 2018 - Dec 2018

I built category classifier on Amazon's dataset (~200,000), utilizing Sci-kit Learn's LinearSVM and extracting features from review text and purchase history. The classifier achieved accuracy over 80% and ranked 2nd out of 355 submissions on Kaggle.
I developed a collaborative filtering based recommendar system for purchase and visit prediction on both Yelp (~1,500,000) and Amazon's datasets. The system achieved above 75% accuracy and ranked top 12% on Kaggle.

Supply Chain and Natural Disaster, Research

Research Assistant• Oct 2018 - Present

I helped economics PhD candidate analyze relationship between natural disaster and global supply chain. I applied natural language processing algorithms to match location data of disasters with that of global geographical ID database, and reported accurate matching rating. I designed automation tools to help statistically describe disasters' impact on global supply chains in 150+ countries.
